Cornell Animal Health Hackathon 2025

What is it?

"This interdisciplinary event brings together students from across degrees, majors, and schools at Cornell University! Teams comprised of veterinary, business, engineering, and design students, come together to create solutions to needs in veterinary health care. On Saturday, mentors provide feedback and guidance to teams. On Sunday, the hackathon culminates in a project showcase to an audience of peers, mentors, and company representatives."
