Material Details

Wheels (Casters): Rubber or polyurethane with a metal core, 3-5 inch wheels with a locking mechanism. Estimated cost per wheel: $5–$10, total cost for four wheels: $20–$40.

Steel Frame: Mild Steel (~$2–$3/kg, $20–$45 total) or Stainless Steel (~$4–$6/kg, $40–$90 total). Estimated weight per unit: ~10–15 kg.

Sling (Fabric & Padding): Options include Nylon, Polyester, or Mesh for durability and comfort. Additional memory foam padding for high-end models. Estimated cost: $10–$25 per unit.

Fasteners & Coating: Includes bolts, nuts, washers, welding materials, and powder coating. Estimated cost: $10–$20.
